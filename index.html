Swamiji and Shivanjali

It is a simple story of one man whose matter-of-fact teachings and unconditional love inspired thousands around the world to embark on an attempt to Serve Love and Give.

Disciple of Swami Shivananda of Rishikesh, His Holiness Swami Shanthanand Saraswathi, is the reason and rhyme behind Shivanjali and its multi faceted service-oriented organizations across the world.

Born in Thirukoviloor on April 7th 1934, Swamiji, as he is fondly called, is a Sadhu/Saint by temperament. Young and old were drawn to the brilliance of his knowledge, his affectionate disposition and his soul-stirring Sathsangs.

Teri Zaathe Paak Kuraana mein
Teraa Darsha Veda Puraana mein
Guru Grantha ji ke Bakaana main
Tu Prakaasha Apna Dikha raha

…sang the bard in a mellifluous voice that resonates the universal truth: “Follow any path, religion or doctrine, but believe in it sincerely, because the sincerity of your belief will illuminate and guide you to the ultimate common goal.” Swamiji attained Mahasamadhi on 27th July 2005 at Coimbatore, where his eternal presence is observed, and considered a center for prayer and meditation today.

Temple of Fine Arts

The Temple of Fine Arts (TFA) is a cultural and service oriented organization with centers in Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Chennai (Madras), Singapore, Perth and Coimbatore. It is guided by the principle – “Art, just for the love of it”, and the belief that any art form is a sure path to the divine. Each music/dance production, practice or everyday class of TFA strives to reflect the above spirit both from the teacher and the students.

TFA imparts classes in Dance (BharataNatyam, Odissi and Contemporary), Music (Carnatic and Hindusthani), Veena, Sitar, Guitar, Violin, Flute, Keyboard, Mridangam, and Tabla to hundreds of students in each of its centers across the globe.

TFA has staged several mega dance productions based on mythology and fiction and conducted mammoth orchestras that audiences around the world have high regard for. The institution believes that there should be no barrier between a student and their quest for the arts. Thus, the fee structure is nominal and scholarships are offered to deserving students.

TFA is recognized as an organization committed to the promotion of art and culture and the classes are taught with the intention of grooming the students in a well-rounded and holistic manner.
